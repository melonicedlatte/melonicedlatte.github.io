## 1. 문제

https://www.acmicpc.net/problem/14425

## 2. 풀이

~~~python
N, M = map(int, input().split())
N_set = set()
answer = 0
for _ in range(N):
    N_set.add(input())
for _ in range(M):
    if input() in N_set:
        answer += 1
print(answer)
~~~
