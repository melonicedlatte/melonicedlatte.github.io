## 1. 문제

https://www.acmicpc.net/problem/11022

## 2. 풀이

~~~python
for i in range(int(input())):
    a,b = map(int, input().split())
    print('Case #%d: %d + %d = %d' % (i+1, a, b, a+b))
~~~
